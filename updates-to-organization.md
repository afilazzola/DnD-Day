What is the Time period?
Forgotten Realms (specifically the Sword Coast) during the late 1480s to mid-1490s DR

What content is allowed?
Official 5e DnD Material including Unearthed Arcana, Eberron and Tasha's Cauldron of Everything

How are characters created?
Please come with characters pre-made. If you need help making a character reach out. Point buyign is preferred.

What character should I make?
Use any of the allowed content and aim for a character level 7.

Who has say on what I can do?
Although everyone is participating in a massive campaign, you are engaging primarily with your DM. The DM will guide you through shared objectives and decide what can happen, what enemies you face, and whether you succeed

What do I design as a DM?
As a DM you can follow the objectives and the genreal story, but you can have free license to introduce whatever challenges against the adventurers.
